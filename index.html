What is Quizuma?

Quizuma is an app that turns pictures of textbook pages into interactive quizzes. It's perfect for fast study and review.

How does it work?

  1. Take a photo of your textbook page
  2. Upload it to Quizuma
  3. Get instant questions & answers

No account needed. Fast, simple, and powerful for students.
